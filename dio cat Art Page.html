Dio: Scarf
Art
This painting is of my cat, Dio, who I rescued from under a truck. I love to paint him and display my artwork on the fridge. This piece is special because it's my first time trying a new painting style. I decided to give Dio a scarf to create a fun 90s look, which adds character to the piece. In my art, I focus on capturing the personality and charm of Dio. I wanted the colors to feel warm and inviting, making anyone who sees it smile. The style I chose is more relaxed and playful, with soft brush strokes that bring out the texture of his fur. By experimenting with this new approach, I aimed to make the painting feel lively
