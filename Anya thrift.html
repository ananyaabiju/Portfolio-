Anya: Thrift Brand
This was one of the branding projects where I decided to create an Indian thrift shop, Anya. The name stands out in a market where few brands focus on this unique concept. Our logo, a threadball, symbolizes the blending of diverse cultures and styles while highlighting our commitment to craftsmanship and sustainability. Each garment is carefully selected, connecting people through their pre-loved clothes. Anya invites you to explore the beauty of Indian fashion and the stories behind every piece.
